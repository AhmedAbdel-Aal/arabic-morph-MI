Representation runs are saved under:
  /workspace/arabic-morph-MI/results/REP01_*

From your local machine, download them with:
  rsync -avz root@<RUNPOD_HOST>:/workspace/arabic-morph-MI/results/REP01_* ./results/

Or:
  scp -r root@<RUNPOD_HOST>:/workspace/arabic-morph-MI/results/REP01_* ./results/

These folders contain hidden_representations.npz files. They are intentionally gitignored.
